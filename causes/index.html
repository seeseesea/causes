The Joseph and Vera Douglas Family Foundation, Inc is a non-profit organization commited to changing communities through education, advocacy, and communtiy service. Our primary area of focus is Jamaica West Indies. We provide services and programs in the United Sates and other Carribean countries (as requested and resources are available). Each year we complete a mission trip that focuses on one of our core areas: health, literacy, and women's entrepreneurship.
